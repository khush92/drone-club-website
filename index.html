About Us

MITAOE Drone Club is a student-run organization at the Maharashtra Institute of Technology (MIT) Academy of Engineering (AOE), Pune, India. We are a passionate group of individuals who share a common interest in drone technology and its applications.

Our mission is to promote the use of drone technology in various fields such as agri culture, transportation, and surveillance, among others. We aim to provide a platform for studentsto learn, experiment and explore drone technology, thereby enhancing their skills and knowledge.

As a club, we organize various workshops, seminars, and events to educate and inspire students about drone technology. We also participate in national and international drone competitions, where we showcase our skills and knowledge.

Join us to be a part of an exciting journey in the world of drone technology and its endless possibilities
